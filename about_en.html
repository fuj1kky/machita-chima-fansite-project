About this site

日本語版はこちらから

This site is an unofficial fan site that aims to support the activities of Chima Machita, a virtual lifer belonging to ANYCOLOR Inc.'s NIJISANJI Project. This is a site where you can browse through the archive of Machita's live performances, read her profile, and find out more about her. I created this site as a part of my study of web development.

Operation of the site

The operation is done by deploying from Github to Netlify, without using a server.Please note that due to Netlify's specifications, it may be slow to load.

Site Features

Profile

You can view a brief profile of Chima Machita. If you'd like to know more about her, click on the Youtube or Twitter links to take a peek at what she's been up to.

Stream Archive

You can view the archives of the live feeds you have done so far. You can also narrow down the delivery by the delivery genre chosen by the creator, and view the set list of the song delivery. You can play the archives directly from this site by pressing the play button.

Songs

You can see a list of song videos, along with information about the people involved in their production. If you are new to Chima Machita, you might want to start here.

Information

This is a collection of various information about Chima Machita. It is scheduled to be updated as needed. You can filter the information by genre, such as goods and voice.
